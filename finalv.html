Admission Process

Our comprehensive evaluation process ensures we select the best candidates

1

Application Submission

Candidates fill the Application Forms with details of their academic records, test scores, and required documents

2

Personal Interview (PI)

Candidates are shortlisted for PI based on their performance in CAT 2024, academics, and work experience, amongst other criteria

3

Final Selection

Final Selection will be done based on a composite score consisting of parameters like CAT 2024 score, academics, work-experience, and the candidate's personal interview scores
