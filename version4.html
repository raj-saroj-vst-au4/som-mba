Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet our program requirements

Academic Requirements

  • Bachelor degree with at least 60% marks
  • Valid CAT/GMAT/GRE score
  • Final year students can apply with latest available marks

Work Experience

  • Work experience is not mandatory but preferred
  • Internships and projects are considered valuable
  • Quality of experience is given more weightage than duration

International Students

  • TOEFL/IELTS score required
  • Equivalent degree certification needed
  • Valid passport and visa requirements

Additional Requirements

  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Updated resume/CV

Selection Process

Our comprehensive evaluation process ensures we select the best candidates

1

Document Screening

Initial evaluation of academic records, test scores, and required documents

2

Shortlisting

Candidates are shortlisted based on academic performance and CAT/GMAT/GRE scores

3

Written Ability Test

Assessment of written communication and analytical skills

4

Group Discussion

Evaluation of interpersonal skills and team dynamics

5

Personal Interview

In-depth discussion on various aspects including academics, work experience, and career goals
